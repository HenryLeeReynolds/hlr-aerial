HLR Aerial — Asset Files
=========================

Place your media files in this folder with the following names:

HERO VIDEO
----------
  hero-video.mp4  — Your showreel or best drone footage (autoplays muted on the homepage)

PORTFOLIO IMAGES
----------------
  portfolio-1.jpg  — Residential property shot (Real Estate)
  portfolio-2.jpg  — Landscape aerial shot (Landscape)
  portfolio-3.jpg  — Event aerial coverage (Events)
  portfolio-4.jpg  — Commercial property shot (Real Estate)
  portfolio-5.jpg  — Coastal or scenic flyover (Landscape)
  portfolio-6.jpg  — Sports or gathering coverage (Events)

TIPS
----
  - Images: 1200x900px or larger, 4:3 aspect ratio works best
  - Video: MP4 format, H.264 codec, 1080p recommended (keep under 20MB for fast loading)
  - Optimize images before uploading (tinypng.com is free)
